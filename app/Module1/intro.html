Introduction to Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, especially computer systems. These processes include learning, reasoning, and self-correction.

History of AI

  • 1950s: The birth of AI.
  • 1960s: AI research begins.
  • 1980s: Rise of machine learning.
  • 2000s: AI becomes mainstream.

Applications of AI

  • Natural Language Processing
  • Robotics
  • Computer Vision
  • Expert Systems

Future of AI

The future of AI holds the promise of more advanced and autonomous systems, impacting every industry from healthcare to transportation.
